Financial Literacy Modules for Students

1. Budgeting Basics:

  • Understanding income and expenses.
  • Creating a budget plan.
  • Tracking expenses and adjusting budgets.

2. Saving and Investing:

  • Importance of saving money.
  • Different types of savings accounts.
  • Introduction to investment options like stocks, bonds, and mutual funds.

3. Understanding Credit:

  • What is credit and why it matters.
  • Types of credit (credit cards, loans, etc.).
  • Building and maintaining good credit score.
